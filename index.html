About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Trung Hieu HOANG. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. My research advisor is Prof. Minh N. Do. I am currently interested in computer vision, deep learning, AI in healthcare, and medical image processing problems. Prior to UIUC, I obtained my bachelor's degree at the University of Science, VNU-HCM under the supervision of Prof. Minh-Triet Tran.
